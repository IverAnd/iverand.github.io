About Me

My name is Ivar Anderson and games have been a passion of mine since I was a little kid. The ability to travel new worlds and learn immersive stories has had a profound impact on my life and has forever shaped my goals in life. I received my undergraduate degree in Gaming, Interactive Media and Mobile at Boise State University as a first generation student. I enjoy learning and never want to stop learning new things.
